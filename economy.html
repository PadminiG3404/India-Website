  • Major industries include IT, pharmaceuticals, and textiles.
  • Strong service and manufacturing sectors.

Major Economic Sectors

The Indian economy is driven by diverse industries.

  • Agriculture - Employs over 40% of the workforce.
  • Industry - Manufacturing, construction, and mining.
  • Services - IT, finance, healthcare, tourism.

Trade & Commerce

India is a key player in global trade.

  • Top exports: petroleum, gems, software services.
  • Top trading partners: USA, China, UAE, EU.
  • Strong presence in global outsourcing and IT services.

Economic Reforms

India has undergone significant economic reforms.

  • 1991 Liberalization - Opened markets to global trade.
  • GST - Simplified tax structure.
  • Make in India - Boosted manufacturing.

Future Economic Prospects

India is poised for strong economic growth.

  • Predicted to be the 3rd largest economy by 2030.
  • Increasing digital economy and startups.
  • Renewable energy and infrastructure investments.
