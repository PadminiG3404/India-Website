History of India

Journey through the ancient, medieval, and modern history of India.

Ancient India

India's ancient history dates back to the Indus Valley Civilization and the Vedic Period.

  • Indus Valley Civilization - One of the world's oldest urban civilizations.
  • Vedic Age - Birth of Hinduism, Sanskrit, and early kingdoms.
  • Mahajanapadas - Rise of powerful kingdoms like Magadha and Kuru.
  • Maurya and Gupta Empires - Golden age of India with rulers like Chandragupta Maurya and Ashoka.

Medieval India

The medieval period saw the rise of powerful empires and the spread of new cultures.

  • Delhi Sultanate - First major Islamic rule in India.
  • Mughal Empire - Great rulers like Akbar, Shah Jahan, and Aurangzeb.
  • Vijayanagara Empire - A powerful Hindu kingdom in the South.
  • Marathas - Rise of Shivaji and resistance against Mughal rule.

Modern India

India under British rule and the road to independence.

  • British East India Company and colonial rule.
  • 1857 Revolt - India's first war of independence.
  • Formation of the Indian National Congress.
  • Mahatma Gandhi and non-violent resistance.

Famous Monuments

India's rich history is reflected in its architectural marvels.

  • Taj Mahal - Symbol of love.
  • Qutub Minar - Tallest brick minaret.
  • Red Fort - Seat of Mughal power.
  • Hampi - Ruins of Vijayanagara Empire.
